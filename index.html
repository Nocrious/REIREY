About me

Designing digital work that feels clear and human.

Greetings! I’m REI, a freelance artist offering creative design services. I specialize in designing user interfaces for websites, systems, and mobile applications using Figma. I am also a digital illustrator with nearly six years of experience, and I continue to improve and develop my skills. My artworks are primarily created in semi-realistic and anime styles, which you may have already seen on my portfolio page. If you are interested in working with me or commissioning my services, you may contact me through any of the platforms listed on the Contact page. Thank you for visiting my portfolio!
